About MovieMagic

Our Project

MovieMagic is a college project developed over the span of four semesters as part of our Computer Engineering curriculum. The idea was born from our passion for cinema and our desire to use technology to help others discover high-quality, lesser-known films.

Under the valuable mentorship of our professor, we focused on building a smart recommendation engine that prioritizes personalized suggestions based on user preferences, rather than mainstream popularity. This project allowed us to blend our love for movies with practical software development experience.
